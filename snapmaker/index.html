Frequently Asked Questions

The questions I get most about Snapmaker printers.

Is the U1 better than Bambu Lab for multi-color?
If you care about filament waste, yes. The U1's tool-changer means zero purge towers — the AMS system on Bambu wastes 30-50% of filament on multi-color prints. Over time, that waste adds up to more than the printer's cost difference.
How hard is the U1 to set up?
Mostly pre-assembled with guided touchscreen calibration. Expect 20-30 minutes from box to first print. Easier than most kit printers, similar experience to Bambu Lab.
Can the Artisan actually replace a dedicated CNC?
For light to medium projects, yes. The 200W CNC module handles wood, acrylic, and soft metals. For heavy aluminum or steel work, you still need a dedicated machine. But for 90% of makers, it's more than enough.
Does Snapmaker have a warranty?
Yes — standard warranty covers manufacturing defects. They also offer a 30-day price guarantee and Snapmaker Care extended plans. Check their site for current terms.
What filament does Snapmaker recommend?
They sell their own line (PLA, PETG, TPU, Silk PLA, PETG HF, TPU 95A HF). But any standard 1.75mm filament works great — Polymaker, Overture, Hatchbox all tested and confirmed.
